About Tarteel Al Quran

Tarteel Al Quran is about getting together and being able to finish the Quran in Ramadaan. We will read one Juz per day, every day for 30 days. Everyday 3 different people will share any lesson they learned from the juz we read that day. There will be about 20 people in the class so everyone will read one page. To make the flow of the class easy we will assign everybody a number and we will read in that same order everyday. If you click the Student tab all the students names will be posted, when you click on your name it will show what page you are assigned everyday until the end.
